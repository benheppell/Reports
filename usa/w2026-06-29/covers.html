Why this sits alongside the GA4 dashboard. The weekly dashboard counts GA4 sevenrooms_booking_complete, which only fires for bookings made through our own website widget. It does not see OpenTable + Resy bookings, and only partially captures Google Reserve. This week is the July 4th holiday, so both GA4 widget bookings (393, -22% WoW / -31% YoY) and total covers created (3,017, -32% WoW / -12% YoY) are down — a genuine seasonal demand dip, not just a measurement gap. OpenTable + Resy covers held broadly flat YoY (561 vs 563), while the widget/other channels drove the softer week. The much steeper GA4 decline vs total covers still reflects the measurement gap: a large share of demand books through channels GA4 never sees.
