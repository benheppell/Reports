Why this sits alongside the GA4 dashboard. GA4 is becoming steadily less reliable as cookie consent and privacy tracking cut what it can see, so treat it as a guide to overall website performance rather than a count of bookings. GA4 sevenrooms_booking_complete only fires for bookings made on our own website. It cannot see OpenTable + Resy, only partly captures Google Reserve, and never sees walk-ins or the reception desks. OpenTable + Resy covers are 931 this week, from 670 the same week last year, roughly 1.4x. So while GA4 reads ▼25.2% YoY, total covers created are +3% YoY. This report is the more accurate view of what we are actually creating.
