Why this sits alongside the GA4 dashboard. The weekly dashboard counts GA4 sevenrooms_booking_complete, which only fires for bookings made through our own website widget. It does not see OpenTable bookings, and only partially captures Google Reserve. OpenTable covers have grown to 1,613 this week (from 296 the same week last year, roughly 5.4×). So while GA4 bookings read -6% YoY, total covers created are +43% YoY. The apparent decline is largely a measurement gap, not a demand problem.
