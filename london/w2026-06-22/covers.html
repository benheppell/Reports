Why this sits alongside the GA4 dashboard. The weekly dashboard counts GA4 sevenrooms_booking_complete, which only fires for bookings made through our own website widget. It does not see OpenTable bookings, and only partially captures Google Reserve. OpenTable covers are 1,572 this week (from 307 the same week last year, roughly 5.1×). So while GA4 widget bookings read -9% YoY, total covers created are +34% YoY. The apparent GA4 decline is largely a measurement gap, not a demand problem.
