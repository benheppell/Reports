Aqua London — Monthly Performance

June 2026 (full calendar month) · GBP · MoM vs May 2026 · YoY vs June 2025 · six venues, reported separately
The read. Across the six London venues, June delivered 47,765 dined covers — down 5% on May but up 15% YoY, almost entirely on Dim Sum Library, which has roughly tripled year-on-year post-rebrand (3,972→10,728). Aqua Shard remains the volume anchor at 14,696 covers, and Shiro (+15% YoY) and Azzurra (+22% YoY, on the Jazz Night push) are the other bright spots; Hutong and Regent St softened month-on-month. Paid media spent £61,156 in June (£31,937 Meta + £29,219 Google). One measurement caveat carried throughout: Google's reported "bookings" are its own SevenRooms conversion attribution (view-through included) and run far higher than GA4 last-click — they are directional efficiency signals, not additive covers.
