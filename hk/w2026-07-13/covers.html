Why this sits alongside the GA4 dashboard. GA4 is becoming steadily less reliable as cookie consent and privacy tracking cut what it can see, so treat it as a guide to overall website performance rather than a count of bookings. It only fires for bookings made on our own website, so it cannot see OpenRice, only partly captures Google Reserve, and never sees walk-ins or the reception desks, which together are over 40% of Hong Kong covers. GA4 reads ▲9.1% YoY while total covers created are +26% YoY. Read this as the fuller demand and channel picture, not as a contradiction of GA4.

Covers = SevenRooms covers by booking-created date (sr_created_at, Asia/Hong_Kong), net of cancellations and deleted bookings. Bar seating areas excluded; private dining and afternoon tea included. Named-host bookings are excluded from this report: large groups and private dining keyed in by the events and reservations teams are not a marketing channel, so they are removed from the covers total rather than parked in an "Other" bucket.
