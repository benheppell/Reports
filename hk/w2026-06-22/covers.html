Why this sits alongside the GA4 dashboard. The weekly dashboard counts GA4 sevenrooms_booking_complete, which only fires for bookings made through our own website widget. It does not see OpenRice bookings or walk-in and reception bookings, and only partially captures Google Reserve. OpenRice covers are 1,783 this week, around 18% of all covers and broadly flat year on year, while walk-in and reception bookings make up roughly half the total. Total covers created were 9,754, +16% YoY, against GA4 widget bookings up +28%. The takeaway is to read them together: GA4 for the website funnel, covers for true demand and the channel mix.
