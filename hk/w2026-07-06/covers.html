Why this sits alongside the GA4 dashboard. GA4 is becoming steadily less reliable as cookie consent and privacy tracking cut what it can see, so treat it as a guide to overall website performance rather than a count of bookings. It only fires for bookings made on our own website, so it cannot see OpenRice, only partly captures Google Reserve, and never sees walk-ins or the reception desks, which together are over a third of Hong Kong covers. GA4 reads ▲7.3% YoY while total covers created are ▲21% YoY. Read this as the fuller demand and channel picture, not as a contradiction of GA4.
