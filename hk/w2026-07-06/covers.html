Why this sits alongside the GA4 dashboard. The weekly dashboard counts GA4 sevenrooms_booking_complete, which only fires for bookings made on our own website. It does not see OpenRice and only partially captures Google Reserve, and it can never see walk-ins or reception bookings, which are roughly half of all Hong Kong covers. GA4 bookings are ▲7.3% YoY but total covers created are ▲20.7% YoY, so the website number understates how much demand actually grew. Read covers as the fuller demand and channel picture, not as a contradiction of GA4.
